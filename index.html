Services

How we engage.

Flexible structures built around what your company actually needs — from embedded fractional leadership to focused project sprints.

Fractional COO

Embedded, ongoing operational leadership on a part-time basis. Ideal for companies that need a senior operator but aren't ready for a full-time hire. Typical engagement: 2–3 days per week.

Operational Assessments

A structured diagnostic of your operations, team, and systems. Delivered in 30–45 days with a clear prioritized roadmap. Ideal for pre-fundraise or post-acquisition clarity.

Team & Org Design

Structuring roles, reporting lines, and hiring plans for your next stage of growth. Includes org design, job architecture, and compensation benchmarking guidance.

Systems & Process Design

Identifying and fixing operational bottlenecks. Building scalable workflows, implementing the right tooling stack, and creating the operating cadences your company needs.

Board & Investor Prep

Preparing management teams for board scrutiny, due diligence, and fundraising cycles. KPI dashboards, board reporting, and ops narrative development.

Interim Leadership

Stepping into a seat on a temporary basis during a transition, departure, or search. Available for COO, VP Ops, and Chief of Staff roles across short or extended timelines.
